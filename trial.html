INTRODUCTION

The name, MUSTAPHA, as you might already have guessed, is of the Arabic origin, and it translates to Chosen one in literal terms.
From this, you can infer that The MUSTAPHA'S literally translates to the chosen ones.

The members belonging to this family have being known to stand out in whatever realm of human endeavour they find themselves in.
Although, some might argue that they owe their great feats to the name they bear (calling it pure luck), it is a better and more sensible argument that it is by the mercy of thy LORD, ALLAH, and their little struggles.

We'll cover that as we unravel the mystery
